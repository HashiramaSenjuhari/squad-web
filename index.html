Color Week

We celebrated Color Week. During that week, each day, we chose one color to wear, fostering a sense of unity as we observed our peers dressed in the same color.

Ethnic Day

We celebrated Ethnic Day, where all of us peers wore traditional dresses to share our cultures. We engaged in various cultural activities, making it a delightful day as we observed our peers dressed in their traditional attire and enjoyed singing songs together.

Fun Activity

One of our squad's specialties is selecting enjoyable activities that truly embody the essence of 'Fun.' Our favorite activity is playing cricket inside our classroom. Engaging in such fun activities not only enhances our physical and mental well-being but also contributes to the development of good sportsmanship skills.
